FAQ

Can I make booking via phone?

No. You cannot place an order via telephone. You can only place an order online

Can I get full cash back after cancellation of order?

No, after a set of period if you cancel the booking some percent of amount will be deducted and rest amount will be transferred to your wallet.

Can I use my wallet amount in other purchasing?

No, you can’t use your calypso wallet amount in other purchasing. You can only use the amount in making booking in calypso app.

What kind of payment methods do you accept?

We accept following payment methods…

a) Online payment (Credit card, debit card, Net banking)
b) Offline booking (you can make booking online and pay at outlet)
c) Partial payment (applicable in party booking)
